Time Adventure

Time passes, people grow, and they come and go.
Things change and yet nothing is different from it's origin.

Seeds planted at the start,
they cycle through, and live.
But no matter how they grow or how much they grow,
they are still the same little seed that was taken care of and is well nourished.

Only that new perspectvies were placed,
was when change was taking form.

Wheather that's the seed's persepective of the world around it,

or the nourisher's view of it's seed,

Everything stays, but still changes.
